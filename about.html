We our the next generation of technology

Tech Meet is home to the head of this generation of AI (Artificial Intelligence). Tech Meet is also a Springhall British School product so if you want to join the team you must be in Springhall British School. Not done! You must be in Year 6, Year 7 or Year 8. Tamunoopu Isaac Ben-Kalio is the leader for this team even-though, he joins Year 9, 10 etc. Eric C. Aduku is the resource and web manager, which means if it wasn't for him, this website you are seeing here will not even exist. Michael Okpor, or as students call him Mr. Michael, is the head of the ICT department of the School and also teaches kids about programming and thanks to him this website also was created.

It was in 2022/23 that Eric Aduku and Tamuno Ben-Kalio collaborated on a coding team called Tech Meet. The two of them were in Year 6 so this team was built by Year 6s for Year 6, Year 7s and Year 8s. In the 2022/23 session, when the dual were in Year 6, the team was assembled, approved and secure. Thanks to Tamuno being a media prefect it was easier to assemble the team. A media prefect is a school prefect that deals with anything media and technology, that was a pretty big job as a child. Either way it is to show how Springhall British School encourages responsibility to train Year 6s to be successful in the future.

Tamunoopu Ben-Kalio joined the school in Year 3, while Eric Aduku joined in Year 1(the first grade of the primary school). In the 2022/23 session, a competition held about robotics and was located in Charlotte, North Carolina, United States of America. This was the first competition outside of the country(Nigeria) that invited the school, Springhall British School.

Springhall British School has thirteen subjects which are Maths, English, Science, Hummanities(History/Geography), Art, Music, DT, PE, PSHE, RE, Computing, French. Also there are sixteen classes in the primary school, from Year one to Year six. From Year one to Year four, there are three classes in each year, meanwhile, year five and year six have two classes in each of the classes. These are: Year One Heron, Year One Falcon, Year One Eagle, Year Two Macaw, Year Two Otter, Year Two Seal, Year Three Crane, Year Three Sparrow, Year Three Toucan, Year Four Orca, Year Four Walrus, Year Four Dolphin, Year Five Swan, Year Five Robin, Year 6 Okapi, Year Six Gazelle. The names of the classes were names of animals e.g.: Year Two Otter, Otter is a name of a canivorous mammal, which is an animal.
